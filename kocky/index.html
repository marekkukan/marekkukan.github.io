        about luck detector

        Numbers with üçÄ symbol represent player's luck score.

        Luck score is defined as

        <luck score> = <number of dice a player has> - <number of dice a player should have (with neutral luck)>

        Therefore lucky players have positive luck score, and vice versa.

        Players with 0üçÄ are neither lucky, nor unlucky.
